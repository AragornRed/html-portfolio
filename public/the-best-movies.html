The Best Movies According to Rafal

My top 3 movies of all time

I must admit that I am not a huge movie fan. I mean I watched plenty of films over the years, however I always was much more into video games, books or music. So to make a list of even three movies, is a little bit hard for me (it would be a lot easier to make a list of the best 3 video games or rock/metal albums). Nevertheless if I have to choose than here's my list

Star Wars (the Old Trilogy of course)

Yes I know it should be only one movie, however for me THIS IS ONE GREATEST MOVIE OF ALL TIME. Trully masterpiece and I mean both - all three parts together and each one of them separetelly. I cannot say which one I like the most, all of them are on exact same level. I also like the prequel trilogy (sic!), however of course not that much as the old one. About the 3rd trilogy (you know - the one which starts with "Force Awakens" movie) - OMG, this is is an abomination of the whole franchise.

Back to the Future trilogy

What else to say - the same as with Star Wars.

Spirited Away/Howl's Moving Castle

I love anime and when it comes to full anime movies, there are no better specialists than Hayao Miyazaki and Studio Ghibli. I cannot really tell which one is better, however when you saw and liked Spirted Away, you should also watch Howl's Moving Castle.
